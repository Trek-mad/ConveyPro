## How It Actually Works

### **Current Workflow (Before ConveyPro):**

1. Client calls solicitor: "How much for conveyancing?"
2. Solicitor/receptionist asks questions over phone (15 minutes)
3. Takes notes, promises to call back
4. Staff member manually calculates LBTT using online calculator
5. Staff member manually calculates legal fees based on price bands
6. Types everything into Word document
7. Finds T&Cs document, attaches to email
8. Emails quote to client
9. **Total time: 45-60 minutes per quote**
10. Client often shops around to 3-4 other solicitors
11. Lowest price usually wins

### **New Workflow (With ConveyPro):**

1. Client calls solicitor: "How much for conveyancing?"
2. Receptionist/paralegal logs into ConveyPro dashboard
3. Creates new quote, fills in form while on phone with client:
   - Property address
   - Property price
   - Property type (house/flat)
   - Transaction type (purchase/sale)
   - Client name and email
   - First time buyer? (affects LBTT)
   - Additional property? (triggers ADS tax)
4. **System instantly calculates:**
   - Full LBTT breakdown (all tax bands shown)
   - Legal fees (based on their custom pricing)
   - Total costs with VAT
5. Staff previews the quote on screen
6. Attaches any additional documents:
   - Terms & Conditions
   - Service guide
   - Client care letter
   - Office brochure
7. Clicks "Send to Client"
8. Professional branded PDF + documents emailed instantly
9. **Total time: 5-8 minutes per quote**
10. Client receives comprehensive quote package
11. Cross-selling emails start automatically
